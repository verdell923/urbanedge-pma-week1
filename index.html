Operations & Fulfillment

Orders are processed through an automated fulfillment pipeline that synchronizes online inventory with warehouse stock, optimizes carrier selection, and tracks shipments end-to-end. This demonstration storefront aligns with the UrbanEdge process modeling and analysis project.

Data-Driven Merchandising

Product assortments and replenishment decisions are informed by demand forecasting, basket analysis, and seasonal trends. This ensures that high-velocity items, such as joggers and hoodies, remain available with minimized overstock.
